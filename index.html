Established for Excellence

Founded in October 2024, Riafuna Fresh emerged with a clear mission: to supply customers with the finest quality fruits and vegetables suited to their specific needs. As a 100% black female-owned business, we bring passion, dedication, and innovation to the fresh produce industry.

Our strategic location near Joburg Market enables us to source high-quality produce efficiently and reliably. We're committed to being the leading supplier of fresh produce by delivering exceptional quality, outstanding service, and maintaining the highest standards of hygiene.

Looking ahead, we're expanding into retail packaging services, working directly with local farmers to bring farm-fresh produce to retailers across Gauteng with tailored solutions for each client's unique requirements.
